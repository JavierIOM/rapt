Cold Crash Mode

The Cold Crash button (snowflake icon, top-right) is used when intentionally dropping fermentation temperature to clarify your beer. When enabled, it ignores low temperature warnings below the minimum safe temperature, preventing false alarms during cold crashing. High temperature warnings still apply for safety.
